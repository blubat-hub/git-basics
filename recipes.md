# My Recipes

## Kimchi Fried Rice
- Cold rice (day old is best)
- Kimchi, chopped
- Sesame oil
- Egg
- Green onions

Fry kimchi in sesame oil, add rice, top with fried egg.

## Korean Corn Cheese
- Canned corn, drained
- Mozzarella cheese
- Mayo
- Sugar (small pinch)
- Green onions

Mix corn with mayo and sugar, top with cheese, broil until bubbly.
